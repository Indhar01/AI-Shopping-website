How We Started

Welcome to our story! Our journey began in [2022] when Hari, a Student, had an idea to create a Negotiator Ecommerce. With a passion for Alibaba, Hari teamed up with Indhar to make it happen.

From small shop, we worked tirelessly to make our vision a reality. We faced many challenges along the way, from heavy competition in surrounding area, but we never gave up.

Through hard work, dedication, and a commitment to excellence, we grew our business and expanded our offerings to include sell products in effective price. Today, we are proud to be a leading ecommerce site, serving customers from Chennai,Sivagangai,Rameshwaram,Coimbator,Pondicherry and beyond.

Thank you for being a part of our story. We look forward to serving you and helping you find the many middle class people will benefit fro this.
